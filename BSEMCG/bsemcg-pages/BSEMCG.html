The Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing with Specialization in Game Development (BSEMCG) is a dynamic and specialized program that combines the fields of entertainment, multimedia, and game development. The Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing with Specialization in Game Development (BSEMCG) is a dynamic and specialized program that combines the fields of entertainment, multimedia, and game development. This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to create immersive gaming experiences and interactive multimedia applications.

With a focus on practical and hands-on learning, BSEMCG students engage in project-based coursework, where they work individually and in teams to develop games and interactive media projects. They gain experience in using industry-standard tools, technologies, and programming languages, allowing them to create high-quality and market-ready games.

By the end of the BSEMCG program, graduates are prepared to pursue careers as game developers, game designers, multimedia specialists, or pursue further studies in related fields. They possess a comprehensive understanding of game development principles, industry practices, and the ability to contribute to the ever-evolving world of gaming and multimedia entertainment.
