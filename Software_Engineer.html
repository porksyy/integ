Software Engineer


A profession responsible for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications or systems. They use their technical expertise and programming skills to create software solutions that meet specific requirements and address business needs.

They work closely with other team members, such as software developers, project managers, and quality assurance engineers, to create and implement software solutions that meet specific requisites and objectives.
