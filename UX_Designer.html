UX Designer


A profession in which focuses on creating and enhancing the overall experience that users have when interacting with a product, service, or system. The goal of a UX, a.k.a. User Experience, designer is to ensure that the user's needs, goals, and preferences are met effectively and efficiently.

IThe primary objective of a UX designer is to ensure that users can easily navigate and interact with a given brand, making their experience enjoyable, efficient, and intuitive. To achieve this, UX designers employ various research, design, and evaluation techniques.
