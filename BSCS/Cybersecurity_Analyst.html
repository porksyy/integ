Cybersecurity Analyst


It is a profession responsible for protecting computer systems, networks, and data from potential security breaches or cyber threats. Their role involves analyzing, monitoring, and responding to security incidents to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

It's important to continually enhance your skills and stay updated with the latest cybersecurity trends, technologies, and threats. Professional certifications such as Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) or Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) can also help validate your expertise in the field.
