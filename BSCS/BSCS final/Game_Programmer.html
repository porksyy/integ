Game Programmer


A profession a professional responsible for developing and implementing software code for video games. It is an exciting and rewarding career path for those who have a passion for both gaming and technology. They work closely with a team of designers, artists, and other developers to create interactive gaming experiences.

They work on implementing the game mechanics, artificial intelligence, physics, graphics, and audio systems.
