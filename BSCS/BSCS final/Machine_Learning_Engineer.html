Machine Learning Engineer


It is a profession who specializes in designing, implementing, and maintaining machine learning systems. They possess a strong understanding of data science and programming concepts, combined with a deep knowledge of machine learning algorithms and techniques.

Machine learning engineers play a crucial role in developing and deploying intelligent systems that can learn from data and make accurate predictions or decisions. They work on various stages of the machine learning pipeline, including data collection, preprocessing, feature engineering, model selection, training, optimization, and deployment.
